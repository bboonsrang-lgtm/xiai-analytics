Xi ai Analytics is a research-focused application that helps authorized users examine TikTok content performance, engagement patterns, and audience trends for educational and non-commercial research purposes.

Access limited to authorized project users

About Xi ai Analytics

The application supports the Xi ai digital-character research project. It provides a structured environment for reviewing authorized TikTok account data and converting performance information into research-oriented summaries and insights.

Purpose

Xi ai Analytics is designed to support content evaluation, audience understanding, academic learning, and responsible research into digital communication and character-based social media content.

Who can use it

Access is limited to the project owner and specifically authorized research users. The application is not offered as a public consumer analytics service and does not sell TikTok user data.

Core functions

The application organizes authorized account information into clear, research-focused views.

01

Performance overview

Review available content metrics and identify changes in performance across authorized posts.

02

Content comparison

Compare content formats and posting patterns to support evidence-based content evaluation.

03

Research summaries

Organize authorized analytics into understandable summaries for educational and research use.

How data access works

  • Data is accessed only after an authorized TikTok user grants permission.
  • Access is limited to the permissions approved by the user and TikTok.
  • Data is used only for the purposes described on this website.
  • Users may revoke authorization through their TikTok account settings.
  • Xi ai Analytics does not sell personal data.
