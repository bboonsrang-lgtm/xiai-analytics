Xi ai Analytics Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 20, 2026

1. Introduction

Xi ai Analytics is an independent, research-focused application operated by the Xi ai Analytics project owner in Thailand. This Privacy Policy explains how information is accessed, used, protected, retained, and deleted when an authorized user connects a TikTok account to the application.

By authorizing Xi ai Analytics to access permitted TikTok information, you acknowledge the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

2. Scope of this policy

This Privacy Policy applies to:

  • The Xi ai Analytics public website;
  • Authorized connections between Xi ai Analytics and TikTok APIs;
  • Information processed for educational and non-commercial research analytics.

This policy does not apply to TikTok’s own data practices. TikTok processes information under its own privacy policy and platform terms.

3. Information we may access

Xi ai Analytics accesses TikTok information only after the TikTok account holder provides authorization. The exact information depends on the permissions approved by the user and TikTok and may include:

Account and profile information

  • TikTok user identifier;
  • Display name, username, and profile image;
  • Public profile information made available through approved API permissions.

Content and analytics information

  • Authorized video identifiers and metadata;
  • Video titles, descriptions, publication dates, and public links;
  • Available performance metrics such as views, likes, comments, and shares;
  • Aggregated content and engagement trends made available through approved APIs.

Authorization information

  • Access tokens and related authorization records required to connect the application;
  • The permissions granted and the status of the connection.
Xi ai Analytics does not request TikTok passwords. Users authorize access through TikTok’s official authorization process.

4. Information collected by the public website

The public informational website does not provide advertising, sell user information, or intentionally collect information through forms.

The website is hosted through GitHub Pages. The hosting provider may process limited technical information, such as IP address, browser type, or request logs, according to its own policies and security practices.

5. How information is used

Authorized information may be used to:

  • Authenticate and maintain an authorized TikTok connection;
  • Display content performance information to authorized project users;
  • Compare content formats, publication patterns, and engagement outcomes;
  • Prepare aggregated or research-oriented analytical summaries;
  • Maintain application security and investigate technical problems;
  • Comply with TikTok requirements and applicable law.

Information is not used for unrelated advertising, automated credit decisions, employment decisions, or the sale of personal data.

6. Authorization and lawful processing

TikTok information is accessed only with the authorization of the relevant account holder and only within permissions approved through TikTok. Users may decline authorization or revoke it at any time.

Where applicable, information is processed on the basis of user consent, the operation of the authorized service, legitimate security needs, and compliance with legal or platform obligations.

7. Information sharing

Xi ai Analytics does not sell or rent personal information.

Information may be disclosed only when necessary:

  • To infrastructure providers supporting secure application operation;
  • To authorized research personnel who require access for the stated project purpose;
  • To TikTok when necessary to operate or secure the API connection;
  • When required by law, legal process, or a valid government request;
  • To protect the security, rights, and integrity of users or the application.

Authorized research reporting should use aggregated or de-identified information where reasonably possible.

8. Data retention and deletion

Information is retained only for as long as reasonably necessary to provide the authorized analytics function, complete the stated research purpose, maintain security records, or meet applicable legal obligations.

Access tokens are no longer used after authorization is revoked and will be deleted or rendered unusable as part of the disconnection process. Identifiable TikTok information that is no longer required will be deleted or de-identified within a reasonable period.

Aggregated or de-identified research results that can no longer reasonably identify an individual may be retained for educational and research records.

9. Security

Reasonable administrative and technical safeguards are used to protect information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or loss. Access is limited to authorized project users.

No system can guarantee absolute security. Users should protect their own TikTok and email account credentials and promptly report suspected misuse.

10. Your choices and rights

Depending on applicable law, users may request:

  • Information about data associated with their authorized connection;
  • Correction of inaccurate information;
  • Deletion of information no longer required;
  • Restriction or objection to certain processing;
  • Withdrawal of authorization and disconnection from TikTok.

Users may revoke the application’s access through their TikTok account settings or contact Xi ai Analytics using the details below.

11. Children’s privacy

Xi ai Analytics is intended for authorized adult project and research users. The application is not directed to children and does not knowingly seek to collect personal information from children.

12. International processing

Information may be processed through infrastructure located outside the user’s country. Reasonable safeguards will be used where required by applicable law.

13. Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated to reflect changes in the application, TikTok API requirements, research practices, or applicable law. The effective date displayed at the top will be revised when material changes are made.
